FAQ



What is a bounce house?

A bounce house is an inflatable structure designed for children to jump and play in.

Are bounce houses safe for kids?

Yes, when used correctly and supervised, bounce houses are generally safe for kids.

What types of bounce houses are available for rent in Clarksville, TN?

There are various types, including traditional bounce houses, combo units, water slides, obstacle courses, and more.

How do I rent a bounce house in Clarksville, TN?

You can rent a bounce house by contacting a local rental company, usually online or by phone.

What information do I need to provide when renting a bounce house?

You'll typically need to provide your name, contact information, event date, and location.

How much does it cost to rent a bounce house in Clarksville, TN?

Prices vary depending on the type of bounce house and rental duration. Expect to pay between $100 and $300 per day.

Are delivery and setup included in the rental price?

It depends on the rental company. Some include delivery and setup, while others charge extra.

Can I set up a bounce house in my backyard?

Yes, if you have enough space and meet safety requirements. Check with the rental company for specific guidelines.

Are there any weight or age restrictions for using bounce houses?

Yes, there are usually weight limits and age restrictions. Check with the rental company for their specific rules.

Are bounce houses cleaned and sanitized between rentals?

Reputable rental companies clean and sanitize bounce houses between rentals to ensure safety and hygiene.

What safety measures should I take when using a bounce house?

Ensure proper supervision, follow all guidelines, and keep sharp objects, food, and drinks away from the bounce house.

Can I rent a bounce house for indoor events?

Yes, you can rent smaller bounce houses suitable for indoor events, such as community centers or gymnasiums.

How far in advance should I book a bounce house rental?

It's best to book as early as possible, especially during peak rental seasons, which may be several weeks in advance.

What happens if the weather is bad on the rental day?

Rental companies often have cancellation policies for bad weather. Check their terms and conditions.

Do bounce house rentals come with insurance?

Some rental companies offer insurance as an option, but it may not always be included in the base price.

Can adults use bounce houses?

Most bounce houses are designed for children, but there are larger units designed for adult use.

What is the duration of a typical bounce house rental?

Rentals usually range from 4 to 8 hours, but longer rental periods are often available for an additional fee.

Can I rent multiple bounce houses for the same event?

Yes, you can rent multiple bounce houses and other inflatables for larger events.

How can I ensure the bounce house is securely anchored?

Rental companies will typically secure the bounce house using stakes or weights to ensure stability.

Can I decorate the bounce house for my event?

Some rental companies allow decorations, but they should not obstruct safety features or damage the bounce house.

What should I do in case of an emergency or equipment malfunction?

Contact the rental company immediately for guidance and assistance.

Do I need to provide power for the bounce house blower?

Yes, you'll need access to a standard electrical outlet to power the blower that inflates the bounce house.

Are bounce houses noisy?

The blower can produce some noise, but it's typically not disruptive.

Can I rent a bounce house during the winter months?

Yes, bounce houses are available year-round, but indoor rentals may be more suitable in cold weather.

What is the cleaning process for bounce houses?

Rental companies clean and sanitize bounce houses using safe and approved methods, typically with commercial-grade cleaners.

Are bounce houses suitable for children with allergies?

Bounce houses are generally safe, but parents of children with allergies should inquire about cleaning procedures.

Can I rent a bounce house for a school or church event?

Yes, bounce houses are often rented for school and church events, and many rental companies offer special rates for these occasions.

What is the cancellation policy for bounce house rentals?

Cancellation policies vary by company. Be sure to inquire about it before booking.

Can I rent a bounce house for a birthday party?

Yes, bounce houses are a popular addition to birthday parties and can be themed to match the party's theme.

How early will the rental company deliver the bounce house?

The delivery time varies, but most companies aim to set up bounce houses well before the event starts.

Can I move the bounce house after it's set up?

Generally, bounce houses should not be moved once they are inflated due to safety concerns.

Are bounce houses suitable for toddlers and infants?

Some rental companies offer smaller bounce houses designed for younger children.

Do I need to provide supervision for the bounce house?

Yes, it's crucial to have adult supervision while children are using the bounce house.

Are there any restrictions on the location of the bounce house setup?

Rental companies may have specific location requirements, such as a flat surface and adequate space.

Can I rent a bounce house overnight?

Some rental companies offer overnight rentals, but it may come with an additional fee.

How should I store the bounce house after the rental period ends?

Follow the rental company's guidelines for deflation, folding, and storage, which typically involve keeping it dry and clean.

Can I rent a bounce house for corporate events?

Yes, bounce houses can be rented for corporate events to add an element of fun and entertainment.

Do I need to sign a rental agreement?

Most rental companies require customers to sign a rental agreement that outlines terms and conditions.

Can I rent bounce houses on holidays?

Yes, many rental companies operate on holidays, but availability may be limited, so book in advance.

What payment methods do bounce house rental companies accept?

Payment methods vary, but most companies accept credit cards, cash, and sometimes checks. Be sure to confirm with the rental company.
